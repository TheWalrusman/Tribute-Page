The Life of Patty

  • 2010 - Is born in Missouri
  • 2014 - Wins dog show medal for dock diving
  • 2015 - Has her first litter of puppies
  • 2016 - Goes on to win multiple medals at shows around the state
  • 2017 - Retires and goes to live a peaceful relaxing life
  • 2020 - Still loves to show off her dummy fetching skills and lounging around all day
